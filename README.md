# IBM-Data-Science-Capstone-SpaceX
![Side 1](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Drook93/IBM-Data-Science-Capstone-SpaceX/main/Side%201%20Image_page-0001.jpg)

SpaceX is an innovative company that has disrupted the space sector by offer a rocket launch specifically Falcon 9 for as low as 62 million dollars; while other providers offer rocket launches for greater than 165 million dollars. Much of these cost savings come after SpaceX has pioneered the concept of reusability by allowing the first stage of the rocket to land for re-use on the next rocket launch, radically disrupting the price of rocket launches. Continuation of this reusable computing will continue to lower the price of launches. As a data scientist of a startup business poised against SpaceX, the end product of this project will be a machine learning pipeline to predict the landing outcome of Falcon 9 - the first stage of any rocket. This is important work to ensure an appropriate price can be bid against SpaceX rocket launches.
